Trader Joe's Lobster Bisque Pasta

This weeknight hack turns Trader Joe's Lobster Bisque into a rich, restaurant-style pasta in about 20 minutes. It's great for date night or an easy upgrade to pantry pasta. Serves 2 generous portions.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz dried pasta (linguine or spaghetti)
  • 1 carton Trader Joe's Lobster Bisque (about 17 oz)
  • 1-2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced (optional)
  • ¼ cup heavy cream (optional, for extra richness)
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • Salt & black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley or chives, chopped (for garnish)
  • Grated Parmesan, to serve

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and cook pasta until just al dente. Reserve ½ cup pasta water; drain.
  2. In a skillet over medium heat, melt butter. (Add garlic and sauté 30 seconds if using.)
  3. Pour in the lobster bisque and simmer 2-3 minutes to warm through. Stir in cream if using.
  4. Toss in the pasta, adding splashes of reserved pasta water until the sauce coats the noodles.
  5. Season with lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Plate and top with Parmesan and herbs.
